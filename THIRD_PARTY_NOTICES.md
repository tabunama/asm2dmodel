asm2dmodel – Third-Party Notices / Attribution

This distribution contains compiled extensions derived from the the ASM2d reactor, combiner, carbon combiner, metal combiner, hydraulic delay, and 1D settler components provided within the BSM1 Matlab®/Simulink® implementation and adapted into Python extension modules.
